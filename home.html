Motivation: Why Analyze Online Reviews?

  • Feedback from customers is essential to decision making.
  • Make companies look trustworthy.
  • Gets your companies name out there.
  • Clear impact on sales.

What we hope to achieve?

Our goal is to help companies get feedback from customers and improve there services on the companies end. This will overall help the company recieve better reviews, which will get there name out there, make them more trustworthy, and increase sales to help the company expand!

Tech Stack Used

    Visualization: Dash, Plotty

    Web Scraping: Selenium, Beautifulsoup

    Data Analysis: Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib

Analytic Tool

Our Customer Review Analytic tool will help Bank Of America improve their services by allowing them to get feedback from customers and overall help the company grow.

We gathered over 1500 reviews from various reliable websites into one application, classifying them by product, service, date and publisher.

DoubleAI analytic tool builds an interactive data visualization of all the reviews scraped from the web! A report of the reviews is created which can be sent to a list of selected BoA representatives for review!
